parent. The cached `_vns` / `_vnc` properties are NOT serialized;
they are re-computed on first access after deserialization and after

upstream behavior and is a deliberate design choice to avoid stale
cache artifacts across restart.

verifies the recompute-bell contract at production scale. No new
