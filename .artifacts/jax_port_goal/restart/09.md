## Public Compatibility Surface

`B2Energy`, `NetFluxes`, `SquaredMeanForce`, `LpCurveForce`, `LpCurveTorque`,
and `SquaredMeanTorque` remain legacy `Optimizable` wrappers that depend on
their downstream `Coil` / `RegularizedCoil` / `Current` parents. Item 09 does
not change `as_dict` / `from_dict` for any of these classes. The constructor
signatures remain identical to the parent commit `a9da18fac` and to the
upstream `1b0cc3a96063197cdbdd01559e04c25456fbe6ff` heads.

The fork-only pre-compute layer (`_SharedCoilState`, `_CoilStateEntry`,
`_CoilStateGroupCache`, spec bindings, DOF-map specs at
`src/simsopt/field/force.py:313-820`) is runtime cache only. It is not
serialized. The shared `_SharedCoilState` service instances are tracked in
`_SHARED_COIL_STATE_SERVICES` (a `weakref.WeakValueDictionary`) so they are
garbage-collected with the owning `Coil` objects across process boundaries.
Pickling or restart never observes this cache; it is rebuilt lazily on first
`_J_args` call after re-instantiation.
