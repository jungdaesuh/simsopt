# Item 10 Restart And Serialization Check

Status: complete for CPU/JAX item 10.

## Public Compatibility Surface

`BiotSavartJAX` is a thin `Optimizable` wrapper around the immutable
grouped coil spec; the wrapper itself is not serialized through a new
schema. Existing public surfaces consumed by this item:

- `simsopt.field.coil.Coil(curve, current)`, `Current`, `ScaledCurrent`,
  `CurrentSum`, `coils_via_symmetries` — the public coil-graph API. Item
  10 does NOT modify `coil.py` core semantics. The legacy `as_dict` /
  `from_dict` shape on the upstream `Coil`, `Current`, `Curve`, and
  `Surface` objects remains the public restart format.
- `simsopt.field.biotsavart.BiotSavart` — the CPU C++ parity oracle.
  Explicitly NOT modified.
- `BiotSavartJAX(coils)` — the wrapper rebuilds its immutable spec at
  construction from the passed coil list. There is no `BiotSavartJAX`
  restart JSON; restart for a `BiotSavartJAX` reconstruction goes
  through reloading the upstream coils via the public `Coil` /
  `Current` / `Curve` `as_dict` graph and then re-instantiating the
  wrapper. `tests/core/test_load_specs.py::SpecBackedBiotSavartJAX`
  already exercises the spec-backed reload path.
- `integral_BdotN(Bcoil, target, normal, definition)` is a pure
  function; no serialization surface.
- `stage2_target_objective_jax.build_stage2_target_objective` consumes
  a `Stage2PenaltyConfig` plus public Coil / Curve / Surface objects;
  no new restart artifact is introduced by item 10.

## Checks

- Existing `tests/field/test_coil.py` covers `Coil`, `Current`,
  `ScaledCurrent`, `CurrentSum`, `coils_via_symmetries` serialization.
  Item 10 does not change these classes.
- Existing `tests/core/test_load_specs.py::test_load_specs_round_trip`
  covers `BiotSavartSpec` round-trip via `SpecBackedBiotSavartJAX`. Item
  10 does not change `BiotSavartSpec`.
- The new closeout test
  `tests/objectives/test_integral_bdotn_item10_closeout.py` builds the
  coil graph in-memory from `create_equally_spaced_curves` and
  `coils_via_symmetries`, constructs `BiotSavartJAX(coils)`, and
  evaluates `B` and `integral_BdotN`. It does not exercise the
  serialization path and is not expected to.

No restart artifact migration is needed for this item.
