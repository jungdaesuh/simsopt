## Source Audit (current HEAD)

- The three public wrappers cache their state as a `CoilStrain` and a
  `framedcurve` reference, then on each `J()` / `dJ()` call read mutable
  `framedcurve.frame_torsion()` / `framedcurve.frame_binormal_curvature()`
  (JAX arrays) and `framedcurve.curve.gammadash()` (host NumPy array)
  before feeding them into the jit-cached integrand kernel.
- Until this item closed, the host `gammadash()` array was passed
  directly into `_lp_strain_penalty_value`, which triggered an implicit
  host->device transfer under `SIMSOPT_JAX_TRANSFER_GUARD=disallow` (see
  `.artifacts/jax_port_goal/red/08.txt`). The fix introduces a single
  `jax.transfer_guard("allow")` boundary around an explicit
  `as_jax_float64` staging of `gammadash`, mirroring item 01's
  `CurrentPenalty` boundary pattern.
