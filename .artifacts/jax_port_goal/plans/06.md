## Source Audit

- All three surface classes (`SurfaceRZFourier`, `SurfaceXYZFourier`,
  `SurfaceXYZTensorFourier`) have JAX adapters via immutable specs in
  `src/simsopt/jax_core/specs.py` (see
  `make_surface_rzfourier_spec`, `make_surface_xyz_fourier_spec`,
  `make_surface_xyz_tensor_fourier_spec`).
- Both `stellsym=True` and `stellsym=False` paths are covered by the
  existing JAX tests:
  - `tests/geo/test_surface_rzfourier_jax.py` — 42+ tests covering
    SurfaceRZFourier. Default fixture `_make_surface` runs at
    `nphi=9, ntheta=10` for both `stellsym=True` and `stellsym=False`.
    Production-scale fixtures (`_make_hlo_probe_surface` at
    `nphi=17, ntheta=18`, Gauss-Bonnet at `nphi=32, ntheta=33`,
    `nphi=4, ntheta=5` degenerate-surface check) pin
    `stellsym=True`.
  - `tests/geo/test_surface_fourier_jax.py` — 8 test classes, ~50
    parametrized tests for `SurfaceXYZFourier` and
    `SurfaceXYZTensorFourier`. Both `stellsym=True` and
    `stellsym=False` covered, up to `32x32` `stellsym=True` at line
    283 (`TestSurfaceFourierJaxHigherOrder::test_nontrivial_modes`).
  - `tests/geo/test_surface_fourier_jax_cpu_ordered.py` — CPU-ordered
    census twin parity tests, `20x20 stellsym=True` and
    `13x9 stellsym=False`.
